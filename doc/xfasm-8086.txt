xfasm-8086(1)          Crossed Fingers Assembler Manual         xfasm-8086(1)

NAME
       xfasm-8086 - i8086 Instruction Encoder

BRANCH TYPES
       near (intrasegment)
              Confined to CS

       short (intrasegment)
              IP-relative by -128 to 127 bytes

       far (intersegment)
              Not confined to CS

BASES
       [bx+si]
       [bx+di]
       [bp+si]
       [bp+di]
       [si]
       [di]
       [bp]
       [bx]
